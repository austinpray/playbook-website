SaaS Playbooks

Create and implement repeatable processes that will allow your team to hit their metrics and goals.

Problem: Your team is working hard but they are wasting time reinventing the wheel, working on ineffective strategies, or too busy to document their process to others can follow along.

Solution: Playbooks allow you to create a repository of processes and include in in different playbooks to execute multiple strategies across multiple business functions.

Understanding The Hierarchy Of Playbook Terminology

  1. Playbook - top level - one per team
  2. Game Plans - second level - multiple game plans per playbook - shareable
  3. Plays - third level - multiple plays wihtin a game plan - shareable

Each team would have a single playbook they are responsible for keeping up to date and accurate. From there they will create game plans. A team can have multiple game plans wihtin their playbook. Each game plan will be comprised of plays that can be shared acrossed each game plan and other team's playbooks.
